About the wall

We've all been there. Some days you're killing it in the office, and some others, you could write a masterclass in procrastination.

Procrastination has a bad press. If you look it up on the web, you'll find tons of essays describing the disease or tutorials to help you eliminate it. This site is here to celebrate procrastination and help you cope with this temporary lack of energy. Do not feel guilty about your procrastination time, simply enjoy it to the fullest.
